## 1. Main / Library Screen (Show List)
In Car Mode, the standard list of shows (Library) is transformed to maximize legibility for in-vehicle use.

### Visual Architecture
- **Large Typography**: Headlines (Date/Venue) are **36px** (`FontWeight.w900`), supporting text is **26-28px**.
- **Dynamic Card Height**: 
    - **Idle Card**: **146px** (compact but legible).
    - **Active/Current Card**: **222-232px** (grows to show playback details).
- **Inline Playback (Active Card only)**:
    - **Marquee Track Title**: **27px** (`FontWeight.w900`).
    - **Track Progress Strip**: A compact version of the full progress bar (`FruitCarModeTrackProgress`) showing buffered and played segments.
    - **Pulse Indicator**: `FruitCarModeTrackPulse` provides visual feedback of active playback.

### Logic & Layout
- **Date-First Toggle**: Respects `settingsProvider.dateFirstInShowCard` to swap Venue/Date positions.
- **Rating & Badges**: Integrates `RatingControl` (compact) and `SrcBadge` directly into the card rows.
- **Background**: If `fruitFloatingSpheres` is enabled in settings, the `ShowListShell` renders a tiny amount of passive floating spheres.

## 2. Playback Screen
The dedicated playback screen in Car Mode is a specialized "active" UI that replaces the standard playback controls.

### Sections (Top to Bottom)
1.  **HUD (Top Row)**: Toggleable between **Meta** (Rating + Shnid/Source Label) and **Stats** (DFT, HD, NXT, LG). Tapping the row cycles the view.
2.  **Hero Area**:
    - **Venue**: **34px** (`FontWeight.w800`).
    - **Location**: **19px**.
    - **Date**: **25px**.
    - **Track Title**: Huge **46px** marquee (`FontWeight.w900`).
3.  **Progress Area**:
    - **Track Bar**: **16px** thick track with a **28px** circular thumb.
    - **Segments**: Shows background (unbuffered), buffered (secondary color, 50% opacity), and played (primary gradient).
    - **Durations**: Current position and total duration (24px bold).
4.  **Controls**: Large Play/Pause and Skip buttons.
5.  **Upcoming Tracks**: A scrollable list of tracks following the current one.
    - **Formatting**: Descending font sizes (**24px -> 21px -> 19px -> 17px**) and opacities (**0.68 -> 0.48 -> 0.34 -> 0.24**) to indicate depth.

### Background
- **Gradient**: Top-to-bottom surface gradient (`surface` -> `surfaceContainerLowest` -> `surface`).
- **Floating Spheres**: Optional `FloatingSpheresBackground` with `SphereAmount.tiny`.

## 3. Random Integration
Random playback in Car Mode is a first-class citizen integrated into the global navigation.

### Implementation
- **Dice Icon**: Located in the 3rd slot of the `FruitTabBar`. In Car Mode, it receives a **1.7x size boost**.
- **Execution Path**:
    1.  Tapping "RANDOM" sets `showListProvider.setIsChoosingRandomShow(true)` (starts dice roll animation).
    2.  Calls `audioProvider.playRandomShow(delayPlayback: true)` to quickly pick a show without blocking the UI.
    3.  Once picked, the `FruitTabHostScreen` instantly jumps to the **Playback** tab (Tab 0).
    4.  The `AudioProvider` then initiates the actual stream loading.

## 4. Settings Screen
Car Mode isn't just a toggle; it's a configuration preset.

### Header Controls
- **FruitHeader**: Contains a dedicated `LucideIcons.car` button.
- **Automatic Enablement**: Toggling Car Mode "On" automatically enables:
    - **Prevent Sleep** (Keep Screen On).
    - **Fruit Floating Spheres**.
    - **Fruit Enable Liquid Glass**.
- **Interface Conflicts**: In `interface_section.dart`, enabling Car Mode **hides the standard UI Scale toggle**, as Car Mode enforces its own specific `scaleMultiplier` (1.5x) across the app to ensure usability.

---

## 5. Global Navigation (FruitTabBar)
The tab bar anchors the Car Mode experience across all screens.

### Styling
- **Scale Multiplier**: **1.5x** applied to overall height and content.
- **Height**: Calculated as `(48.0 + 32.0) * tabScaleFactor`.
- **Labels**: Standard labels (PLAYING, LIBRARY, RANDOM, SETTINGS) are shown unless `hideTabText` is enabled.
- **Glass Effects**: Uses `BackdropFilter` and `LiquidGlassWrapper` for a premium feel on Web/PWA.

## Potential Challenges for Replication
- **Scaling Complexity**: The use of `tabScaleFactor` and `effectiveScale` across many nested widgets requires careful synchronization to avoid overflows.
- **Marquee Timing**: Marquee velocity and blank space are tuned differently in Car Mode (faster/larger).
- **Tab Transitions**: Car Mode specifically enforces animated transitions between tabs in `FruitTabHostScreen`, even if glass effects are off, to prevent jarring jumps.
