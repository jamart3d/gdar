# Issue Report: Google TV UI v1.1.35 Alignment Check

- **Report Date**: 2026-03-02 11:10
- **Investigated Version**: 1.1.35 (Commit `d7a48e0`)
- **Current App Version**: 1.1.39+139

## Issue Summary
The user requested a verification of whether the **Google TV UI** on the **left side (Show List Screen)** included stars (rating controls) and badges (source counts/SHNIDs) in Version 1.1.35 (`v135`).

## Findings

### 1. Position in Dual-Pane Layout
In v1.1.35, the Google TV interface utilized a **Dual-Pane Layout** (`TvDualPaneLayout`).
- **Left Pane**: Contained the `ShowListScreen` (header and the scrollable list of shows).
- **Right Pane**: Contained the `PlaybackScreen` (track list and playback controls).

### 2. Visibility of Stars and Badges on the Left Side
The investigation into the `ShowListCard` (the component used for list items in the left pane) at the v1.1.35 commit reveals the following:

- **Stars (Rating Controls)**:
    - **Yes**, they were included in the list, but with a specific conditional:
    - Stars only appeared on the card in the list if the show was **currently playing** OR if the show had **exactly one source**.
    - If a show had multiple sources and was NOT playing, the stars were **hidden** in the main list view.
    - Stars were rendered via the `_buildBalancedControls` method, placed on the right-hand side of the card.

- **Badges (Source Count / SHNID)**:
    - **Yes**, badges were present on the cards in the left-side list.
    - Specifically, the `_buildBadge` method rendered the source count (e.g., "1", "3") or the SHNID if enabled.
    - `SrcBadge` was also displayed if a target source was identified (playing or single-source show).

## Conclusion
In **v1.1.35**, the Google TV Show List (left pane) **did include stars and badges**, but the stars were conditional based on playback state or source count. Each card in the list on the left side of the screen displayed these interactive elements in the `controlZone` on the right side of the individual "Pill" card.
