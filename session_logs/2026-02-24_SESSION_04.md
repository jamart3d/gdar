## Hypotheses
The playback screen UI scaling issue on small phones is caused by a restrictive `maxPanelHeight` (40% of screen) in `PlaybackScreen`. When "UI Scale" is enabled or system font scaling is high, the `scaleFactor` increases the height of individual elements (labels, buttons, progress bar). Since `maxPanelHeight` does not grow with `scaleFactor`, the available expanded area shrinks proportionally as `minPanelHeight` grows. This forces the `FittedBox` in `PlaybackPanel` to shrivel the entire controls column to fit within the tiny remaining space.
